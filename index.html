LifeInvader Important Quick Rules

Illegal Items

  • Firearms & Ammunition of any kind
  • Bulletproof vests / Dark armored vests
  • Weed, cannabis seeds/trees, drugs, cocaine
  • EMS surgical/medical masks
  • Vehicle & people scanners (radars)
  • Balaclava masks, Ropes, Lock picks
  • USB virus flash drives, Troll Ads
  • Engine Blocks, Smuggling Machines
  • Offensive text on license plates

Rejection Only

  • Crowbar, All fabric, Animal/Armor skin
  • Head bag (except luminous), Air Horn
  • Barricades, Traps, Poison darts, Army Uniform
  • Tracking sensors, Dangerous razors
  • Body armor, Body armor plates
  • Cocaine ingredients, Paper for money
  • Satellite dish, Forest mushroom tincture
  • First Aid Kits, all pills, Food items

Banned Content

  • Grand Coins, Battlepass tickets
  • Branded armor or Hype Body armor
  • Gangs, Nationality, Unofficial family names
  • Drugs & Medkits/pills
  • Food items (except raw/cooked fish)
  • Birthday or celebratory advertisements
  • Sexual content, hints or prostitution
  • Sale of people, slaves or kidnapping
  • State Org Leaders & deputy leaders

General Rules

  • Format: Start with: Buying, Selling, Trading, Selling or trading.
  • Prices: Buying = Budget:, Selling = Price:. Capitalize first letter, use $. Write out full values (no 'k'/'M').
  • Limits: Max 1 vehicle or 3 items.
  • Banned Places: Mega Mall, Gang HQ, Black Market, Ghetto, State Offices.
